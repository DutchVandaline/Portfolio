Sealant Problem with SafeAI

By Junha Park Published on Dec 13, 2024

Overview

As part of my school co-op program, I’ve been given an opportunity to work on a real-world problem for a company called SafeAI. SafeAI is a company which specializes in solving various challenges related to Artificial Intelligence. The specific task involves the production of silicone sealant, where empty plastic cylinders are manufactured, and brand covers are attached to them. However, during the process of attaching the covers, defects can occur. My objective is to develop a solution to detect defects on the sealant covers.
